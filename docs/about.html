Our Story

Grandma's Kitchen has been serving up homestyle comfort food for generations. Our Whitefield diner opened with a simple goal: to make every guest feel like they just walked into their grandma's house — warm smiles, hearty meals, and memories on every plate.

From buttermilk pancakes to savory pot roast, everything is made with love and care. We’re proud to be a North Country staple, and we’re just getting started!

That 50s Diner Feel

Our cozy booths, checkerboard accents, and friendly staff bring back that classic 1950s diner charm — with a Grandma's Kitchen twist. It's the kind of place where regulars are family and newcomers are always welcome.
